In [[Polar Coordinates]] we have the coordinate transform,

$$
\begin{align}
x &= r \cos \theta \\
y &= r \sin \theta. \\
\end{align}
$$

Thus having a [[Jacobian]],

$$
\left|
\frac{\partial(x, y)}{\partial(r, \theta)}
\right|
= r,
$$

and an [[Area Element]],

$$
\d x\,\d y = r \,\d r \,\d \theta.
$$

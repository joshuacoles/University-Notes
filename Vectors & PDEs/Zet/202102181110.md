By the [[Linearity of the Integral]], we can express the [[Vector Field Product Integral]] as,

$$
\iint_\Omega \F \,\d A = \begin{pmatrix}
\iint F_1 \,\d A\\
\iint F_2 \,\d A\\
\iint F_3 \,\d A
\end{pmatrix}
$$

Which in index notation can be expressed as,

$$
\(\iint F \,\d A\)_i = \iint F_i \,\d A.
$$

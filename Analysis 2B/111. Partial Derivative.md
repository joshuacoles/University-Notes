Suppose,
- ${U \sub \R^n}$ is an [[Open Set]],
- $f: U \to \R$ is a function

Given,
- $x \in U$
- $j \in J_n$

Then

$$
\parfrac{f}{x_j}(x) = \lim_{h\to0}\frac{f(x + he_j) - f(x)}{h},
$$

if it exists is the Partial Derivative of $f$ with respect to $x_j$ at $x$.
